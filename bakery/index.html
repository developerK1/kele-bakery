About KK Bakery

KK Bakery is a home-run artisan bakery owned by Kelebogile "Kele" Selogiloel, based in Gauteng, South Africa. Kele has a passion for baking and takes pride in delivering freshly made scones and biscuits to homes, offices, and small events around town.

Whether you're planning an afternoon tea or need a sweet treat for your family gathering, KK Bakery offers a variety of flavors and batch sizes to suit your needs. All goods are baked fresh to order—100% homemade, 100% delicious.

We take orders via Facebook or WhatsApp, and you can choose to have your baked goods delivered or to pick up from our home-based kitchen.
